Embark on a Journey: Unveiling the Story of Shyama Public School

At Shyama Public School, Gyanpur, our faculty and staff are the heart and soul of our institution. We take immense pride in our team of dedicated educators and supportive staff who not only impart knowledge but also inspire, mentor, and guide our students towards achieving excellence.

Each member of our team is carefully selected for their expertise, passion, and commitment to nurturing young minds, ensuring that every classroom is a space for innovative learning and holistic development. State-of-the-Art Infrastructure

Explore our premium facilities designed to support educational and physical growth.

Modern Classrooms

Our classrooms feature multimedia projectors, ergonomic seating, and innovative design that creates a truly interactive learning experience for every student.

Innovation Spaces

Our innovation hubs offer state-of-the-art environments where eager students explore creative projects, experiment with ideas, and collaborate on makerspace designs.

Science Labs

Our advanced science labs, equipped with modern instruments and safety protocols, foster hands-on exploration, chemistry/physics experiments, and innovation.

Computer Labs
